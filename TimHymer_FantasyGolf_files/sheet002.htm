Rules
Entry Fee $TBD

Selection of pro players will be completed at an approved location prior to the event
A draw will be held and each participant will pick six pro players one at a time according to the draw. Only The major tournaments will have the additional team awards based on the top three players of each participant.

Sidebets are an important and traditional activity but are unsanctioned and self-governing. The usual and customary bet is $5.00 on top player versus top player for all tournaments. Sidebettors must pay and collect on their own.

NOTE: AT THE CONCLUSION OF THE GOLF SEASON THERE WILL BE SCHEDULED PAYOFF. SIDEBETS NEED TO BE HONORED AT THIS TIME. IT WILL BE UP TO SIDEBETTORS TO PAY/COLLECT ON THEIR OWN. IF YOU CANNOT ATTEND THIS MEETING FOR ONE REASON OR ANOTHER-YOU WILL HAVE A TWO WEEK GRACE PERIOD TO HONOR YOUR BETS. IF THIS NOT DONE-YOU WILL NOT BE INVITED BACK NEXT YEAR!
